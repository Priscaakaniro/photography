What is photography?

Photography in simple terms, is the act of capturing light, either digitally (via a digital camera or a phone),
or with film (with a film camera), to produce a photograph.
There are numerous reasons why people take photos, it could be for business reasons, a hobby, or for keepsake.

For whatever reason anyone takes pictures for, the primary reason is clear. For storytelling.
Photographs are a way of telling a story, it is also the perfect way to go back in time and relieve moments from the past.
There are several types of photography: portraits, landscape, wildlife etc.
