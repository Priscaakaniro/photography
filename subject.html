Identifying your subject

A lot of things or people can be in a picture, but a photo subject is the main component of the photo.
A good photograph has a clear and distinctive subject.
A photo without a clear subject can make the photo look rough, rushed, and unplanned.
while a photo with a clear and distinct subject can make a photo look organized,
planned and overall, aesthetically pleasing.

In a picture, a simple test can be done to ascertain whether the subject is clear or not.
When you look at a picture, at first glance, where is your eye drawn to?
Where your eye is drawn to at first glance should be the subject of your photo.
To get a clearer shot of your subject, consider changing your position,
or changing the angle you are shooting from.
